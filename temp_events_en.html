- What is this contest?

This contest was created to strengthen and expand the game community, making their wishes and dreams come true. In this contest, players can create almost any tuning and vinyl for a new car, which will be released a little later, when the contest ends. The only rules are that the vinyl should not contain anything illegal, obscene, and should not be the cause of various conflicts.

- What do you need to do to participate in it?

1 - Go to the telegram channel @A_Games_Studio

2 - In the corresponding post of the competition there is a picture with a new car.

3 - Download the picture and in the image editor paint the car as you like!

4 - Upload a photo under the post with a signature to remain in the history of the game, if you wish, you can offer your price tag for the car, and write the history of the creation of the tuning kit for the car.

- How to win the competition?

1 - The one who makes the most beautiful art will win.

2 - The winner will also be influenced by likes from community.

- What prize will the participants get?

The winner - His car will remain in the game forever, when examining his car in the garage there will be his authorship (for example: Idea "autogamer"), also, if his work also has a story, then next to the inscription there will be a button which will show a panel with a text history of the car. Also a promo code for free receipt of his tuning set and 10,000 rp.

For participants and regular players - a promo code for 50,000 in-game currency and 2,500 rp.

- How long will the competition last?

Depending on the number of participants. If there are too few (less than 5 people) before July 5, then the competition will last for a week. If after that there are still too few participants, the reward will be reduced by 2 times. If there are no participants at all, then no one will receive awards.
